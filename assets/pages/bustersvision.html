BUSTER'S VISION

INTRODUCTION

When I began seeking more design opportunities to pair with my previous development job, Buster's Vision advertised the need for a graphic designer on volunteermatch.org.

I inquired, and created social media graphics advocating for their Puppy Mill Stomp campaign. The Puppy Mill Stomp campaign's mission is to spread awareness to the public about the dangers and cruelties that occur as a result of puppy mill breeding.

The images below are the resulting images used on their Facebook, website, and various volunteers' instagram accounts.
